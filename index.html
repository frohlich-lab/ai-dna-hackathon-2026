Overview

About the event

This Crick-funded hackathon focuses on the practical application of generative AI to biological challenges in DNA and genomics. It features two participation tracks — a Set Problem Track with predefined genomics challenges built around the Alphagenome API, and an Open Innovation Track where participants tackle their own biological questions with greater freedom to combine tools.


The event brings together staff and students from Crick partner universities (UCL, King's, Imperial) and external participants, fostering collaboration between AI researchers and biologists. The goal is to showcase the potential of AI in genomics while enabling beginners to integrate tools like Alphagenome into their research workflows.

Open Innovation

Open Innovation Track

  • Bring your own biological question
  • Combine Alphagenome with other tools
  • Full creative and methodological freedom
  • Ideal for independent research problems
